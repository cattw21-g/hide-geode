\# Hide Geode Button



A simple, lightweight utility mod designed to clean up your Geometry Dash homescreen by removing the default Geode button from the bottom menu. 



\### Features

\* \*\*Custom Keybinds:\*\* Fully integrated with Geode's native keybind system. PC and Controller players can assign any key (or gamepad button) to open the menu instantly.

\* \*\*Mobile Ghost Mode:\*\* Hides the button to fix your bottom menu layout, but allows you to open Geode simply by tapping the empty \*\*Top-Right\*\* corner of your screen.

\* \*\*Seamless:\*\* Changes update your layout immediately. 



Designed for a cleaner GD experience!
